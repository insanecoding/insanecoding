### Hello world 👋

I'm a Senior Frontend Developer at Prose, which is a custom beauty startup. I have 12 years of experience and a Master's degree in Cybersecurity. I mainly specialize in Javascript/Typescript and the React stack (Redux, Next.js, Webpack). But I'm always open to trying something new. For example, I had to learn Svelte in two weeks to deliver a project, and I enjoyed it.

I'm not afraid of complex and seemingly messy things. I wrote plugins for Webpack/PostCSS/Semantic-Release when the existing solutions didn't match our needs, and it wasn't so difficult at the end 🤭

I'm extremely excited about modern agentic AI (tried them all: Codex, Gemini-CLI, Cursor, Claude Code), but I'm not your AI bullshitter 😛. I know where AI excels and where it hits its limits. 
Also, I don't just apply it to my work. I apply it to my life! That's how I used Gemini to refine my investment strategy. It helped identify a critical correlation between my different assets and swap NASDAQ with an equally-weighted S&P500, wow! Or I relied solely on ChatGPT for my DELF French exam preparation and scored 97.5% for B1. One year later, it repeated it for B2 and got 95%.

Here at Prose, I succeeded in owning our tech stack, architecture, and long-term strategic vision after the departure of our tech lead. I became a go-to person for peer frontend developers. I've been doing code reviews almost every day. I also present a lot (tech demos, Frontend Chapter, tech all-hands) and do peer programming to share knowledge. I know how to mentor. I've grown three developers, and they were all promoted to the next grade (two to Middles and one to Senior).

Also, did I tell you that I like GraphQL? At Omio, I invested a lot into learning all the ins and outs of the Apollo Client. Then, I successfully refactored the data layer of the existing user management app.

I try to give back to the community. I reported issues in [use-async-resource](https://github.com/andreiduca/use-async-resource/issues/1), [size-limit](https://github.com/ai/size-limit/issues/173) and [react router](https://github.com/remix-run/react-router/issues/7374). I made PRs to several webpack plugins: [html-webpack-banner-plugin](https://github.com/insanecoding/html-webpack-banner-plugin) and [inline-manifest-webpack-plugin](https://github.com/insanecoding/inline-manifest-webpack-plugin) and I started open source at Prose by releasing our very first [NPM package](https://www.npmjs.com/package/typescript-coverage). I want to be even more active in future.

Have any ideas or questions? Reach out to me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dmitrii-novozhilov/)

Also, don't hesitate to check my [X](https://x.com/insanecoding) where I write about technologies.
